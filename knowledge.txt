[SOURCE: Parashara Hora Shastra, Chapter 12]
Rule 1: If Jupiter aspects the Moon, it creates 'Gaja Kesari Yoga', giving fame and polite speech.
Rule 2: Saturn's aspect on the Moon causes 'Vish Yoga', creating mental stress, delay in success, but high discipline.
Rule 3: Sun in the 10th House (Meridian) is 'Digbala', giving executive power and government favor.
Rule 4: Mars in the 10th House gives energy for leadership but creates conflict with superiors.
Rule 5: Rahu (North Node) in the 10th House indicates success in foreign lands or software technology.

[SOURCE: Saravali, Chapter 30]
Rule 6: A planet transiting the 3rd, 6th, or 11th from the Natal Moon brings rapid financial gains.
Rule 7: A planet transiting the 8th or 12th from the Natal Moon causes losses or unexpected expenses.
