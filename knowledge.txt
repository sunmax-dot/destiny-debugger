SECTION 3: KEY YOGAS (THE DESIGN PATTERNS)
================================================================================
[SOURCE: Saravali, Ch 30 & BPHS Ch 35]

[YOGA: Gaja Kesari]
- Rule: If Jupiter is in a Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) from the Moon.
- Effect: Gives polite speech, lasting fame, wealth, and victory over enemies. Good for advisory roles.

[YOGA: Pancha Mahapurusha - Ruchaka]
- Rule: Mars in own sign (Aries/Scorpio) or Exalted (Capricorn) in a Kendra house.
- Effect: Born leader, courageous, excellent for Engineering or Military/Police leadership.

[YOGA: Pancha Mahapurusha - Bhadra]
- Rule: Mercury in own sign (Gemini/Virgo) or Exalted (Virgo) in a Kendra house.
- Effect: High intellect, data expert, excellent communicator, statistical genius.

[YOGA: Pancha Mahapurusha - Hamsa]
- Rule: Jupiter in own sign (Sag/Pisces) or Exalted (Cancer) in a Kendra house.
- Effect: Ethical leader, highly respected, purity of mind, strategic wisdom.

[YOGA: Pancha Mahapurusha - Malavya]
- Rule: Venus in own sign (Taurus/Libra) or Exalted (Pisces) in a Kendra house.
- Effect: Wealthy, lover of arts/luxury, charismatic leader, success in design/media.

[YOGA: Pancha Mahapurusha - Shasha]
- Rule: Saturn in own sign (Cap/Aquarius) or Exalted (Libra) in a Kendra house.
- Effect: Stern leader, controls masses, builds massive structures/organizations, long-lasting legacy.

[YOGA: Budhaditya]
- Rule: Sun and Mercury conjoined in the same house (especially 1, 5, 10).
- Effect: "Nipuna" (Expert) Yoga. High intelligence, skill in calculation/coding, successful commerce.

[YOGA: Chandra-Mangala]
- Rule: Moon and Mars conjoined or aspecting each other.
- Effect: Earnings through unscrupulous means or aggressive business tactics. High financial drive.

[YOGA: Vipareeta Raja Yoga]
- Rule: Lord of 6, 8, or 12 is placed in 6, 8, or 12 (e.g., 6th lord in 8th).
- Effect: Success rises from the ashes of failure. Victory through sudden events or competitor's loss.

[YOGA: Kemadruma (Negative)]
- Rule: No planets (except Sun/Rahu/Ketu) on either side of the Moon.
- Effect: Mental anxiety, fluctuations in wealth, feeling unsupported. Needs "cancellation" to fix.

- Effect: Career changes, domestic stress, unexpected obstacles.

- Rule: Jupiter transiting 2nd, 5th, 7th, 9th, or 11th from Natal Moon is AUSPICIOUS.
- Effect: Wealth, birth of children/projects, marriage, spiritual growth, promotion.
- Rule: Jupiter in 1st, 3rd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th, 12th is generally challenging or neutral.
- Effect: Increased expenses, change of place, or heavy workload without immediate recognition.

SECTION 7: STRATEGIC REMEDIES (THE PATCHES)
================================================================================
[SOURCE: Modern Behavioral Astrology]

- AFFLICTION: Weak Sun (Low Confidence/Authority)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Imposter syndrome, fear of speaking in stand-ups.
  - REMEDY: Take credit for your code publicly. Lead a small project. Wear Gold/Ruby.

- AFFLICTION: Weak Moon (Anxiety/Burnout)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Taking code reviews personally, unstable sleep due to on-call shifts.
  - REMEDY: Strict "No Screen" time after 9 PM. Drink water from a silver vessel.

- AFFLICTION: Weak Mars (Lack of Drive)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Procrastination, inability to ship features, fear of conflict.
  - REMEDY: Physical exercise before work. Practice "Tough Love" in code reviews.

- AFFLICTION: Weak Mercury (Communication Bugs)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Misunderstanding requirements, messy documentation, spaghetti code.
  - REMEDY: Write technical blogs. Learn a new syntax. Green plants on desk.

- AFFLICTION: Weak Jupiter (Lack of Vision)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Stagnation, stuck in "Junior" role, lack of mentorship.
  - REMEDY: Teach a junior developer. Contribute to Open Source.

- AFFLICTION: Weak Saturn (Discipline Issues)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: Missing deadlines, ignoring technical debt, hate for process/JIRA.
  - REMEDY: Clean your desk daily. Donate to blue-collar workers. Follow a strict routine.

- AFFLICTION: Rahu Issues (Obsession/Illusion)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: "Shiny Object Syndrome" (switching stacks constantly), over-engineering.
  - REMEDY: Finish one project before starting another. Avoid office gossip.

- AFFLICTION: Ketu Issues (Detachment/ apathy)
  - TECH SYMPTOM: "Quiet Quitting," feeling invisible, brilliant code but no recognition.
  - REMEDY: Meditation. Focus on backend/invisible architecture.
